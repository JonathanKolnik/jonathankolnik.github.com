This is a Whole New Box of Toys Toybox is the new brand that we, Jonathan Kolnik and Brendan Mahony, have recently established. The idea for starting a T-shirt brand first began one boring summer. In previous months, we joked about starting a T-shirt company and selling the shirts out of an ice cream truck but soon we discovered that we should actually go ahead and create one, minus the truck. Now, a year later, two screen printers in the garage, and a complete re-branding of the company, we have created something new and exciting: TOYBOX. Toybox Isn’t Just For Kids We are not trying to create shirts that anyone can buy at large chain stores. We are aiming to make shirts that are limited and unique. We want Toybox to represent who we are and our personal style. Toybox is a fun and playful company that brings the everyday person back to their childhood. Toybox Both of us work together on everything. We design, market, print, and create everything mutually and evenly. Both of us are very excited for the future of Toybox. We will be coming out with new designs all the time and we thank you very much for visiting our site.
